System: unicycle_dynamic_obstacle
Description: Unicycle robot navigating around a moving circular obstacle. The CBF accounts for obstacle velocity to maintain safety. This is a harder scenario where the legacy controller fails more often.

Controllers:
  Legacy: LQR / pure pursuit (nominal, no CBF)
  Evolved: CBF-QP with barrier constraint (robust safety filter)
  CBF type: Distance-based barrier with obstacle velocity compensation

Simulation:
  Time step: 0.07 s
  Duration: 20.0 s
  Samples: 200
  Safety condition: h(x(t)) = ||position - obstacle_position(t)||^2 - obstacle_radius^2 >= 0
  Seeds: Uniform random sampling over input ranges, shared seeds between legacy and evolved

Input Features (6):
  initial_distance_to_obstacle: Euclidean distance from unicycle to static obstacle at t=0 [m] sampled [0.5, 5.0], physical [0.0, 100.0]
  initial_speed: Initial linear speed of the unicycle [m/s] sampled [0.1, 3.0], physical [0.0, 10.0]
  obstacle_radius: Radius of the circular static obstacle [m] sampled [0.2, 1.5], physical [0.01, 5.0]
  initial_heading_error: Difference between unicycle heading and goal direction at t=0 [rad] sampled [-3.14, 3.14], physical [-3.14159, 3.14159]
  obstacle_speed: Linear speed of the moving obstacle [m/s] sampled [0.1, 1.5], physical [0.0, 5.0]
  obstacle_heading: Heading direction of the moving obstacle [rad] sampled [-3.14, 3.14], physical [-3.14159, 3.14159]
